Extreme Sports

Extreme Sports or Adventure sports are a category of sports defined by how extremely dangerous the sports are and the harm they might cause to the athlete or how far the athlete would push themselves. Some examples are Sky diving, Snowboarding, Parkour and Rock climbing, we will discuss these sports and how they are competed on. Sky diving involves a contestant or a group of contestants, Some of the categories are Formation diving where the contestants dive together and form either a predetermined formation or a sequence of different formations, Vertical sky diving is just as the event mentioned previously but done vertically, Speed diving is event where divers dive with the intent to reach the ground the fastest. Moving onto Snow Boarding is considered an extreme sport as it is performed in very cold environment in impressive speeds, snowboarding competition have multiple events competitors compete to win at. Alpine is an event where a snowboarder races down a hill through gates to reach, Halfpipe is a event where snowboarders perform tricks on a halfpipe and are judged based on multiple criteria such as height and difficulty. Parkour competitions commonly have two categories speed and free-running tends to be judge based on the quality and flow of sequence of moves, while speed parkour is focused on the speed at which the obstacle course is completed at, both categories are done on an obstacle course.
